Muzimake Privacy Policy

Last modified on: September 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by Muzimake ("Company," "we," or "us") when you use our website (www.muzimake.com) (the "Website") to purchase a custom song (the "Song"), or when you otherwise interact with our services (collectively, the "Services"). By using the Website, you are agreeing to the practices described in this Privacy Policy and in our Terms of Use.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, we'll revise the date at the top and, in some cases, notify you directly. We encourage you to review this page whenever you use our Services.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information You Provide
When you purchase a Song or interact with our Services, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Payment details (processed securely by a third-party payment provider)
  • Stories, details, or personal content you submit for creating your Song
  • Preferences such as genre, vibe, and occasion

2. Non-Personal Information
We may also collect information that does not directly identify you, including:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system, IP address)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time on site, actions taken)
  • Cookies or similar technologies that help us improve your experience

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Create and deliver your custom Song
  • Process payments and send order confirmations
  • Communicate with you about updates, changes, or support requests
  • Send you promotional offers or newsletters (you can opt out anytime)
  • Improve and personalize the Services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud or misuse

We will not sell your personal information.

Sharing of Information

We only share your information when necessary to provide our Services:

  • With trusted third-party vendors (such as payment processors)
  • To comply with applicable law or protect rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transfer (e.g., merger or acquisition)

We may share aggregated or non-identifiable data with partners for analytics or marketing purposes.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website performance
  • Provide a smoother user experience

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features of the Website may not work properly.

Your Choices

  • Emails & SMS: You may opt out of promotional emails or messages by clicking "unsubscribe" or contacting us.
  • Account Data: You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing us.
  • GDPR / International Users: If you are in the EU or another region with data rights, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using industry-standard security practices. However, no system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the Services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
